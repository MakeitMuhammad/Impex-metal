Who We Are

A Trusted Bridge Between Continents

Impex Metal Oreasia is one of Pakistan’s most established metal trading houses, serving the industry since 1971. We specialize in international scrap metal procurement — sourcing from certified suppliers across the USA, Canada, Europe, Australia, Brazil, Saudi Arabia, and beyond.

We act as your trusted partner — handling every aspect of the deal from pricing negotiation and contract drafting to full documentation, logistics coordination, and after-sale support.

01
End-to-End Deal Management
From initial offer to final invoice — we manage the complete trade cycle including contracts, proformas, and full shipping documentation.
02
BIR & ISRI Compliant
All materials traded under internationally recognized BIR and ISRI grade standards, ensuring quality and regulatory compliance.
03
Direct Payment, No Third Parties
Payments go directly to the supplier — no intermediary holds your funds. Full transparency at every step of the transaction.
